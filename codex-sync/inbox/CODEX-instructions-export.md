# Working Rules

Prepared: 2026-03-31

## Brand Defaults

- premium
- feminine
- disciplined
- intelligent
- direct
- elegant
- emotionally controlled

## Communication Style

- use direct language
- use clean, plain language
- keep paragraphs short to medium
- make decisions instead of presenting too many equal options
- reduce overwhelm fast

## What Good Work Should Do

- clarify the next move
- strengthen authority
- support sales
- protect brand integrity
- turn vague ideas into usable assets
- keep execution simple and repeatable

## Strategy Defaults

- simple offer stack
- realistic execution
- consistency over endless expansion
- authority content over trend chasing
- reusable assets over constant reinvention
- revenue first, then supporting systems

## Decision Rules

- when in doubt, choose clarity over noise
- choose strength over fragility
- choose elegance over cheapness
- choose practicality over theory
- choose real results over trend-driven ideas

## Writing Rules

- concise
- sharp
- elegant
- not fluffy
- not generic
- not fake-hype
- not mass-market coach language

## What To Avoid

- generic fitness or coaching language
- cheesy empowerment copy
- therapy-speak
- corporate jargon
- too many ideas at once
- overbuilt funnels
- weak positioning
- advice that sounds good but is hard to execute

## How To Handle Missing Information

- make the most reasonable assumption
- state the assumption clearly
- keep going
- only stop to ask a question if the answer would materially change the outcome

## Current Priority Order

1. consistent action
2. sales
3. positioning

## Current Blind-Spot Protection

If the work starts getting scattered, simplify, choose one direction, name the tradeoff, and reduce the next move to one to three actions.
