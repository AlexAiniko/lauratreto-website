# Business Context

Prepared: 2026-03-31

## Brand Position

Laura Treto is best positioned as a premium private movement coach and movement specialist in Key West.

She should not be framed as a generic trainer, a loud online coach, or a mass-market fitness personality.

## Core Brand Traits

- strong
- feminine
- direct
- intelligent
- elegant
- disciplined
- premium

The tone should stay clean, clear, and emotionally controlled.

## Main Buyer Groups

### Local private coaching buyer

- adults in Key West, especially 45+
- people who feel stiff, unstable, weak, hesitant, or disconnected from their body
- people who want a more intelligent path than generic workouts
- people in the gap between finishing physical therapy and fully trusting normal training again
- people willing to pay for nuance, observation, and precision

### Digital product buyer

- women 35 to 45
- interested in body recomposition
- willing to follow a structured 12-week plan
- lower-ticket digital buyer compared with the local private client

## Why The Local Offer Is Strong

The local offer is the clearest near-term cash path because it is more differentiated and more trust-based than the digital product.

The strongest angle is the bridge between rehab or physical therapy discharge and confident real-life training.

That angle is powerful, but it should stay as a lead conversion lane, not the entire brand.

## Core Promise

Laura helps adults move with more strength, stability, mobility, and confidence through premium private coaching rooted in movement quality and expert observation.

## Pricing

### Local

- assessment or single session: $125
- 4-pack: $460
- 8-pack: $840

### Digital

- Strong Lean Athletic, 12 weeks: $149 one-time

## Channel Roles

- TikTok: fast authority, movement education, direct visibility
- Instagram: premium authority, visual proof, refined credibility
- Facebook: local repetition, proof, and explanation for warm audiences

## Content Themes That Fit Best

- move well enough to live Key West fully after 45
- the bridge between pain, discharge, and real-life movement
- strength and stability for island life
- what Laura sees that generic coaches miss
- premium private coaching for adults who need nuance
- calm authority, body literacy, and elegant discipline

## Competitive Reality

The business should not try to win on volume, price, hype, or generic transformation language.

The differentiation is:

- premium private coaching
- strong observational skill
- movement quality over noise
- a direct but elegant public voice
- local relevance tied to real Key West life

## Current Strategic Truth

The business should currently optimize for local authority, local bookings, and assessment conversion first.

The digital product matters, but it should not dominate day-to-day decisions until the local machine is moving cleanly.
