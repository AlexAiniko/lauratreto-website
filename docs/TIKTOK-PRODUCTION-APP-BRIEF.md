## 3. Scope Justifications (one per scope, paste into each scope's "Description" field)

### `user.info.basic`
**Why needed:**
> Required to authenticate the business owner's TikTok account via Login Kit and confirm the authorized user matches the @coachlauratretoo account before any API action is taken. Used exclusively for account identity verification at login.

### `video.list`
**Why needed:**
> The automation reads the creator's own video list to identify recent uploads and fetch video IDs. These IDs are then used to query comments on those specific videos. No video data from other accounts is accessed or stored.

### `comment.list`
**Why needed:**
> The system fetches comments on the creator's own videos to identify viewers asking about coaching services, pricing, or booking. This is the input step before any automated reply is generated. Comments are processed in memory only and not stored.

### `comment.create` (most scrutinized — use this exact framing)
**Why needed:**
> The business owner operates a coaching brand with a high volume of TikTok comments asking "how do I book?", "what do you offer?", and "how much are sessions?" This scope allows the system to post a warm, pre-approved reply to those comments on the creator's behalf, directing viewers to lauratreto.com to book a consultation. Replies are generated by an AI assistant trained to match the coach's voice and are reviewed in dry-run mode before production posting. No replies are posted to third-party accounts. This scope is used exclusively for customer service and lead follow-up on the creator's own content.

**Additional note for reviewers (add in the general notes / use case description field if available):**
> We are a solo creator and sole proprietor. comment.create is used only to reply to comments on our own videos asking about our coaching services. Replies are warm, human-toned, and always direct users to our official website. We do not use this scope to spam, promote third-party products, or reply to content we do not own.

### `user.info.stats`
**Why needed:**
> Used to track follower growth, video views, and engagement rate as part of the creator's weekly business KPI review. Data is read-only, displayed only to the business owner in a private dashboard, and never shared externally.
